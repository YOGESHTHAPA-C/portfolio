Yogesh's Receipe for the Best
Cinnamon Rolls

Indgredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 tespoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Mekt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.
    Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After the dough has doubled in size,roll it out a large rectangle.
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix in the sugar and cinnamon.
  8. Spread the butter for the filling onto the dough then roll the dough into a swiss roll.
  9. Cut the roll into 3cm sections and place flat into a bakery tray.
  10. Preheat the oven to 350F or 180C,then bake the rolls for 20-25mins until,
    lightly brown
